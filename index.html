Background Why I got into science.

To say that I enjoy the outdoors in a bit of an understatement. I probably spend more weekends sleeping in a tent or in my car than I do in my own bed. Unfortunately, many of our fragile ecosystems are struggling in the Anthropocene, and if we want to preserve all the things that we get to enjoy as nature, we have a lot of work to do. This is where science comes in for me. By providing a thorough understanding of ecosystemic relationships, science plays a crucial role in making informed policy decisions, and I see myself playing a small but important part in supporting that function.
